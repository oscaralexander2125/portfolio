One Book

-React -Redux -Node.js -Mongo.db

One Book was made to help voracious readers keep track of all of their books they have read, are reading, or will read. It also lets users leave a review to have a note of their personal thought of what they’ve read. The user can leave the book as public and will let other users see recommendations to embark on the next book to read.

Health Tracker

-Node.js -Mongo.db -Express -jQuery

I believe the start of any health journey begins with a goal and a way to keep track of their daily routines. This app helps health enthusiasts keep track of their health habits. Users can record their calorie consumption, energy expenditure and write down their meals so they know what they’ve been eating and how it has affected their weight.

Company Comparer

-HTML -CSS -Javascript -jQuery

Company Comparer is intended for investors looking to compare company ratios. This app is meant to help investors decide which company might be worth investing in by looking at two different companies. It calculates important values such as ROE and debt ratio.
