One Book

-React -Redux -Node.js -Mongo.db

One Book is made to help voracious readers keep track of books they have read, are reading, or will read. It allows users to take notes or leave comments so that they can refer to them later. Users can leave the book as public so that other readers can use the recommendations to embark on their next book adventure.

Health Tracker

-Node.js -Mongo.db -Express -jQuery

I believe the start of any health journey begins with a goal and a way to keep track of daily routines. This app keeps track of health enthusiasts’ habits. Users can record their calorie consumption, energy expenditure, and record their meals. It helps users document what they are eating and compare it to their weight loss goals.

Company Comparer

-HTML -CSS -Javascript -jQuery

Company Comparer is intended for investors looking to compare company ratios. This app is meant to help investors decide which company might be worth investing in by looking at two different companies. It calculates important values such as ROE and debt ratio.
